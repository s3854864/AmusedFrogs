About Us

PowerCore Digital was established by 4 entrepreneurs in 2021. The idea for an online graphics card retail store is anecdotal. The notion surfaced through the founders’ difficulty in sourcing cheap graphics cards catering for cryptocurrency mining. At the time, each founding member was an IT major and had noticed that the problem they faced, was in fact universal at the college. Just like that, within a matter of months, PowerCore Digital was born. The founders had one philosophy: to provide a wide variety of graphics cards designed for cryptocurrency mining at the lowest possible price point.

In April of 2021, the first PowerCore Digital website was commercialised: www.powercoredigital.com. The site offered cards sourced from 6 separate brands, and later began to advertise complementary hardware to students such as motherboards and central processing units (CPU).

Our site offers the world’s leading graphics card brands at the lowest possible cost. We purchase older-model cards from manufacturers at cheap costs and apply a fractional mark-up for sale in the marketspace.

PowerCore Digital is your one-stop shop for cryptocurrency mining graphics cards. We are the only single-line retailer who provides a wide product assortment; designed specifically to meet your need for cheap processing power.

Packaging

A common qualm for many e-tailers is the environmental damage dealt by excessive product packaging. At PowerCore Digital, we recognise the trade-off that exists between environmentally sustainable packaging and product protection in transit. Hence, we package our graphics cards in corrugated carboard and compostable packing peanuts. These two materials are durable, ensuring the protection of delicate graphics cards in their journey across sea and road, while dealing no damage to the environment per se.

Logistics

To minimise carbon emissions associated with delivering PowerCore Digital graphics cards to final consumers, we employ CO2 – Neutral Delivery. When customers elect for standard delivery, we will bulk ship products rather than schedule deliveries for individual sales. This minimises the number of transits, by extension decreasing carbon emissions.

Philanthropy

We engage in philanthropic investment by providing graphics card resources to NFP organisation at even cheaper costs. The hope is that, with the additional resources, the organisations’ programmatic execution will increase.
